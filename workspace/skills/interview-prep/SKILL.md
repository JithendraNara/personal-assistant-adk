# Interview Prep Skill

Comprehensive interview preparation protocol.

## When to Use

- "Help me prepare for [company] interview"
- "Practice behavioral questions"
- "Review my interview answers"
- "What should I expect at [company]?"
- Technical interview preparation

## When NOT to Use

- General job search → use `search_jobs`
- Salary negotiation without interview context
- Resume building → use `generate_resume_suggestions`
- Casual career chat

## Preparation Framework

### STAR Method Coaching
- **S**ituation: Set the context
- **T**ask: Describe the challenge
- **A**ction: Explain what YOU did
- **R**esult: Share the outcome with metrics

### Technical Prep Checklist
1. Review company tech stack
2. Practice system design (for senior roles)
3. Prepare 3 relevant project stories
4. Research recent company news and products
5. Prepare thoughtful questions for interviewers

### Follow-up Protocol
- Send thank-you email within 24 hours
- Reference specific discussion points
- Reaffirm interest and fit
